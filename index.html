About Me

Hi, my name is Andrea Forti and I am a passionate video game programmer.

A lifelong passionate gamer, I am a fan of MMOs, RPGs, survival games, sandbox games, and board games. I have been a Dungeon Master for D&D 5e for 9 years, with experience in world-building across various settings for homebrew campaigns.

I have worked for 4 years as a backend programmer in the banking sector, then I studied at Digital Bros Game Academy as a Game Programmer for 2 years . During this time, I had the opportunity to study Unreal Engine and Unity, and work with many talented students to develop several academic video games using both game engines.

I am currently working as a freelance video game and software developer since late 2024, currently working on Narramor project while continuing the development of the Lo-Friction project and other smaller side projects / prototypes
